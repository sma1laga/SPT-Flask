About Signal Processing Toolkit

The Signal Processing Toolkit (SPT) is an innovative, interactive platform that makes learning and experimenting with signal‑processing concepts both accessible and fun. Hosted by the Lehrstuhl für Multimediale Signalverarbeitung (LMS) at FAU, the toolkit is offered as a licence‑free educational service.

Key Features

  • Interactive Modules: Explore convolution, Fourier analysis, filter design, Bode plots, modulation techniques, and advanced noise‑reduction.
  • Real‑Time Visualisation: Adjust parameters and immediately see results in time‑ and frequency‑domains.
  • Process‑Chain & Block‑Diagram Tools: Build complex signal‑processing chains, edit block diagrams, and visualise direct‑form structures.
  • Continuous & Discrete Modes: Switch between continuous‑time and discrete‑time tools such as dynamic convolution and the DFT.
  • Audio & Speech Processing: Upload audio files, design speech filters, and analyse spectrograms.
  • Training & Exam Modules: Practise with guided exercises and timed assessments with detailed feedback.

Our Vision

Our mission is to empower learners, educators, and professionals by demystifying complex signal‑processing topics. Through interactive visualisation, experimentation, and real‑time analysis, we aim to make advanced concepts approachable—and spark innovation along the way.

Ideal Band-Pass (VL 8-13)

This demo shows an ideal band-pass filter \( H(\mathrm{j}\omega) = \mathrm{rect}\!\left(\frac{\omega}{\Delta\omega}\right) * \big[\delta(\omega-\omega_0) + \delta(\omega+\omega_0)\big] \) in the time and frequency domains. You can adjust the bandwidth \(\Delta\omega\) and the center frequency \(\omega_0\). The envelope of \(h(t)\) is displayed as a dashed curve.
